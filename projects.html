WHO ARE WE?

The Innovation team took off in 2016, as part of the Product team, under the wings of François Langevin. A structural shift in 2018 gave way to a broadening of its horizons, becoming the center of innovation for TD Insurance as a whole. The strength of the team lies in the commitment and diversity of the individuals who make it up. Complementary talents facilitate collaboration, the different ways of thinking stimulate creation, and the thirst for development allows for the continuous challenge of the status quo.
