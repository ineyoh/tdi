ABOUT TDI INNOVATION

We are a multi-disciplinary team in charge of propelling TDI in the innovation space while addressing our clients needs and well-being. We are continually thinking about how to come up with the right solutions for our customer right now and tomorrow.

VISION

To be a disruptive, purpose-driven team that innovates and helps others innovate by focusing on the needs of our customers, communities, and colleagues

PURPOSE

  • Foster a healthy culture of innovation at TDI by outlining the necessary framework to support progress.
  • Identify TDI's problem statement and deliver innovative solutions with speed and agility.
  • Research on current and disruptive trends and develop TDI's innovation strategy and roadmap.
ABOUT THE TEAM

The Innovation team took off in 2016, as part of the product team, under the wings of Francois Langevin. A structural shift in 2018 gave way to a broadening of its horizons, becoming the center of innovation for TD insurance as a whole.
The strength of the team lies in the commitment and diversity of the individuals who make it up. Complementary talents facilitate collaboration, the different ways of thinking stimulate creation, and the thirst for development allows for the continuous challenge of the status quo.
